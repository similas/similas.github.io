About

I am a Machine Learning Engineer working on real-time AI systems, interactive avatars, and production-grade ML pipelines. My work focuses on low-latency inference, system performance, and bridging research with real-world deployment.

Journey

Started programming in 2012 through robotics, competing internationally and earning a Silver Medal at the Austrian Open Robotics WorldCup 2014 (Rescue League). Studied Computer Engineering at QUT, then founded an edtech startup — raised seed funding, shipped 300+ robotics companion units (Roobins), and received a UNICEF innovation award. Completed a Master’s in AI at SBU and worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Azki (Iran’s largest insurtech). Moved to Canada to pursue a PhD in AI @ Concordia University (coursework at Mila), then joined the Vector Institute as a Machine Learning Associate. Currently, an ML Scientist at QARL AI, building real-time interactive digital avatars.
